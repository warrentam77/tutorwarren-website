About Tutor Warren

Helping students understand electrical theory, practical installation and testing through clear explanations, practical learning and interactive study support.

Meet Tutor Warren

Tutor Warren is an experienced Electrical Installation Lecturer, practical instructor and former electrical/plumbing tradesman with a strong passion for helping learners succeed in education and in industry.

With 15 years of teaching experience in universities, FE colleges and schools and over 13 years of practical trade experience, Warren combines real-world electrical knowledge with teaching methods that make difficult concepts easier to understand.

tutorwarren.com was created to support students studying Electrical Installation by turning classroom theory into practical, visual and interactive learning resources that help students build confidence and pass assessments.

What makes Tutor Warren different?

• Real trade experience behind the teaching
• Learning resources designed by a practising educator
• Visual explanations for difficult concepts
• Interactive quizzes and self-study tools
• Helping learners turn theory into practical understanding
