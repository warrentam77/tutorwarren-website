🎯 Level 2

Your Level 2
Maths Topics

Master the skills needed for work and further education. Level 2 is the highest Functional Skills Maths qualification — equivalent to a GCSE grade 4 pass.

🎓 Level 2 is equivalent to GCSE Maths Grade 4. Topics include compound interest, reverse percentages, complex algebra, and advanced data analysis.

Choose a topic below

🔢
Topic 1
Number & Algebra

Work with powers, roots, standard form, complex fractions and introduce algebraic thinking with formulae and equations.

What you'll learn
  • Powers, roots & standard form
  • Recurring decimals & fractions
  • Substituting into formulae
  • Solving linear equations
  • Direct & inverse proportion
💷
Topic 2
Money & Finance

Handle complex financial calculations including compound interest, reverse percentages, tax bands and multi-step financial problems.

What you'll learn
  • Compound interest
  • Reverse percentages
  • Income tax & tax bands
  • Loan repayments & APR
  • Currency conversion
📐
Topic 3
Measures, Shape & Space

Calculate with circles, cylinders and complex compound shapes. Apply Pythagoras' theorem and work with bearings and vectors.

What you'll learn
  • Circumference & area of circles
  • Volume of cylinders & prisms
  • Pythagoras' theorem
  • Complex compound shapes
  • Density, pressure & speed
📊
Topic 4
Data & Statistics

Analyse and interpret complex data sets, use grouped frequency tables, draw and interpret cumulative frequency graphs and histograms.

What you'll learn
  • Grouped frequency tables
  • Estimated mean & median class
  • Scatter graphs & correlation
  • Two-way tables
  • Combined probability
