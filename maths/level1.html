📐 Level 1

Your Level 1
Maths Topics

Build on your Entry Level skills with more complex calculations. Each topic includes clear explanations, worked examples, practice questions and a quiz.

📋 Level 1 is equivalent to approximately GCSE Grade 2–3. Topics cover more demanding calculations with percentages, ratio, algebra and statistics.

Choose a topic below

🔢
Topic 1
Number & Calculation

Work with whole numbers, fractions, decimals and percentages in more complex situations including multi-step problems.

What you'll learn
  • Multiples, factors & primes
  • Fractions of amounts & equivalents
  • Percentages — increase & decrease
  • Ratio & proportion
  • Order of operations (BODMAS)
💷
Topic 2
Money & Finance

Apply maths to financial situations including wages, bills, discounts, interest and more complex budgeting.

What you'll learn
  • Wages, tax & deductions
  • Percentage discounts & increases
  • Simple interest
  • Unit pricing & value for money
  • Profit & loss
📐
Topic 3
Measures, Shape & Space

Calculate areas and perimeters of compound shapes, use scale and work with more complex measurements and conversions.

What you'll learn
  • Area of triangles & compound shapes
  • Volume of cuboids
  • Scale drawings & maps
  • Metric & imperial conversions
  • Speed, distance & time
📊
Topic 4
Data & Statistics

Interpret and draw a wider range of charts and graphs, calculate averages and understand probability at a basic level.

What you'll learn
  • Mean, median, mode & range
  • Pie charts & line graphs
  • Frequency tables
  • Scatter graphs
  • Basic probability
