Electrical Installation Resources

Study support for college learners covering electrical science, practical installation, inspection and testing, regulations and renewable technologies.

Learn step by step

These resources are designed to help students understand not only what to do, but why each step matters. Start with the level you are studying, then move towards testing, fault finding and exam practice.
